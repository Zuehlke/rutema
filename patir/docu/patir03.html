patir 0.3 is out!

Patir 0.3 has been released!
You won't find the bugs unless you use the code.
And while we are fervently working on a release for rutema we have to realise once more that infalibility is not one of our traits.

0.3 fixes a bug in CommandSequenceStatus (a missing strategy attribute) that produced exceptions when nesting stati.
There is also a new formatter for the logger so that the output is less verbose than the default.

Take a look at the rdoc for the library.
And since you're going to want to play with it...just do a

gem install patir

You can also get the gem directly from Rubyforge
