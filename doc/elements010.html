rutema_elements 0.1.0 is out!

rutema_elements 0.1.0 has been released!

The sleekest, easiest and fastest way to add functionality to your parsers.

rutema_elements is a collection of modules with element parsing implementations ready for your use. Full flexibility and configurability with solutions for real world problems.

To get the gem just do: gem install rutema_elements

Usage is extremely simple. Check the introductory documentation and the gem's RDocs for details.
